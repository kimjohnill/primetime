How It Works

1

Consultation

We start with a conversation to understand your goals, brand vision, and product needs. Whether you're building from the ground up or expanding your existing line, our team works with you to create a clear roadmap covering everything from strain selection to packaging style.

2

Create

Once we lock in your vision, our production team gets to work. We handle everything in-house cultivation, extraction, pre-roll production, filling, and professional grade packaging. You'll get high-quality, compliant products that are ready to hit the shelves under your brand.

After everything is packaged and quality checked, we ship directly to your facility or retail partners. Fast turnaround, reliable service, and full transparency at every step. All you have to do is focus on growing your brand and we've got the rest covered.
